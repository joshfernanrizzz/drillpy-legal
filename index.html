About Drill.Py

Educational App

Drill.Py is a mobile learning app that teaches Python data science through short, focused drills. Every concept is followed by a problem to solve — with instant feedback on every answer.

Topics Covered

NumPy

Arrays, indexing, slicing, operations — 7 sessions, 42 drills.

Pandas

DataFrames, filtering, groupby, merging — 8 sessions, 45 drills.

Matplotlib

Charts, subplots, styling — 6 sessions, 36 drills.
